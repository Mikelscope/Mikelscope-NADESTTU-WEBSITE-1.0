About Us

The National Association of Delta State Students at Obafemi Awolowo University (OAU) is a student-run organization that represents the interests of students from Delta State who are enrolled at OAU. The organization provides a platform for these students to come together, network, and engage in activities that promote their welfare and well-being.... The association also serves as a liaison between the students and the university administration, working to address any issues or concerns that may arise. The association also serves as a means of fostering unity among students from Delta State and promoting their cultural heritage. The association is usually run by an elected executive council consisting of a president, vice-president, secretary, treasurer, and other members. They are responsible for organizing events and activities, communicating with members, and representing the interests of the group to the university administration. These activities can include social events, cultural celebrations, community service projects, and advocacy efforts.The association also serve as a resource for students, providing information about university services and opportunities. Additionally, it provides mentorship and networking opportunities for its members, helping them to connect with other students from their home state and prepare for life after graduation.
